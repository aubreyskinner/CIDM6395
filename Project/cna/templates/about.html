About Linkella

At Linkella, our mission is simple: to help connect families with compassionate and qualified Certified Nursing Assistants (CNAs) who are dedicated to providing care in your local community. We understand how important it is to ensure your loved ones receive the best care, and we’re here to make that process easier and more accessible.

Our Story

Linkella was founded with the belief that quality care should be accessible to everyone. With a growing need for personalized healthcare support, especially for those who are elderly or facing medical challenges, we set out to build a platform that bridges the gap between families and trained CNAs in surrounding areas.

What We Do

We provide an easy-to-use platform that connects people in need of care with qualified CNAs. Whether you need someone to help with daily living activities, offer personal care, or provide assistance with mobility, our platform is here to match you with the right professionals who can provide the care your loved one deserves.

Our Values

  • Compassion: We believe that everyone deserves to be treated with dignity and respect.
  • Trust: Our CNAs are thoroughly vetted to ensure they meet the highest standards of professionalism and care.
  • Community: We foster a sense of community by connecting local CNAs with those who need their help most.
  • Accessibility: We’re committed to making healthcare accessible to everyone, no matter where they are.

Why Choose Us?

Linkella makes it easy to find CNAs in your area who are trustworthy, skilled, and ready to provide exceptional care. Our platform ensures that you can quickly find someone who meets your specific needs and schedule. Plus, you can communicate directly with your CNA and access updates on your loved one's care to stay informed every step of the way.

Join Us

If you’re a CNA looking to join our team and provide top-tier care, or if you’re a family member seeking care for a loved one, we invite you to explore what Linkella can do for you. Together, we can ensure that quality care is just a click away.
