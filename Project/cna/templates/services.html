Our Services

Linkella is a platform designed to connect your loved ones with certified nursing assistants (CNAs) in nearby areas, ensuring they receive the care they need, when they need it. Whether you're looking for assistance with daily tasks, personal care, or specialized health support, Linkella makes it easy to find trusted professionals in your community.

1. Connecting Families with CNAs

Our platform simplifies the process of finding qualified CNAs who are available to provide compassionate care to your loved ones. You can search for CNAs based on location, expertise, and availability, ensuring that you find the right person for the job.

2. Personalized Care Matching

Linkella allows you to filter and connect with CNAs who specialize in the specific needs of your loved ones. Whether it’s assistance with mobility, medication management, or companionship, we help you find professionals who match your requirements.

3. Real-Time Availability

Our platform offers real-time scheduling, making it easy for you to book care services when they are needed most. CNAs can update their availability on the platform, ensuring that your loved one receives timely assistance when required.

4. Trusted and Verified CNAs

We prioritize safety and peace of mind. All CNAs on Linkella are thoroughly vetted, including background checks and credential verification, ensuring that you can trust the professionals providing care to your family.

5. Easy Communication and Updates

With Linkella, you can communicate directly with the CNAs providing care. Whether it's to share updates, ask questions, or make adjustments to the care plan, our platform ensures smooth and secure communication between you, your loved ones, and their caregiver.
